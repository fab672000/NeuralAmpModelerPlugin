6 Compute coefficients for 2-path polyphase IIR filter, half-band filter or

21  k = 0 -2
22  1 + a z

25  1
26 H (z) = - (A0 (z) + A1 (z))
27  2
28 
29 Sum of A0 and A1 gives a low-pass filter.
30 Difference of A0 and A1 gives the complementary high-pass filter.
31 
32 For the Pi/2 phaser, product form is (a - z^-2) / (1 - az^-2)
33 Sum and difference of A0 and A1 have a Pi/2 phase difference.

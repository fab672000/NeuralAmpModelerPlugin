\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_canvas_1_1_lattice}{Sk\+Canvas\+::\+Lattice}} divides \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_bitmap}{Sk\+Bitmap}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_image}{Sk\+Image}} into a rectangular grid. Grid entries on even columns and even rows are fixed; these entries are always drawn at their original size if the destination is large enough. If the destination side is too small to hold the fixed entries, all fixed entries are proportionately scaled down to fit. The grid entries not on even columns and rows are scaled to fit the remaining space, if any.

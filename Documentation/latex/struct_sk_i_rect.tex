\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} holds four 32-\/bit integer coordinates describing the upper and lower bounds of a rectangle. \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} may be created from outer bounds or from position, width, and height. \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} describes an area; if its right is less than or equal to its left, or if its bottom is less than or equal to its top, it is considered empty.

{\footnotesize\ttfamily void Sk\+I\+Rect\+::adjust (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{int32\+\_\+t}]{dL,  }\item[{int32\+\_\+t}]{dT,  }\item[{int32\+\_\+t}]{dR,  }\item[{int32\+\_\+t}]{dB }\end{DoxyParamCaption})\hspace{0.3cm}{\ttfamily [inline]}}

Adjusts \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} by adding dL to f\+Left, dT to f\+Top, dR to f\+Right, and dB to f\+Bottom.

If dL is positive, narrows \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} on the left. If negative, widens it on the left. If dT is positive, shrinks \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} on the top. If negative, lengthens it on the top. If dR is positive, narrows \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} on the right. If negative, widens it on the right. If dB is positive, shrinks \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} on the bottom. If negative, lengthens it on the bottom.

The resulting \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} is not checked for validity. Thus, if the resulting \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} left is greater than right, the \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect}{Sk\+I\+Rect}} will be considered empty. Call \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_i_rect_a0b80c7843b17d05794019b88294cb8bb}{sort()}} after this call if that is not the desired behavior.

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
View that displays a traditional piano style keyboard. Finger presses are reported to a \mbox{\hyperlink{interfacecom_1_1mobileer_1_1miditools_1_1_music_keyboard_view_1_1_music_key_listener}{Music\+Key\+Listener}}. Keys that pressed are highlighted. Running a finger along the top of the keyboard will only hit black keys. Running a finger along the bottom of the keyboard will only hit white keys.

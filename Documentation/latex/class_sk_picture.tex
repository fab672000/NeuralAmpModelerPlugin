\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} records drawing commands made to \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_canvas}{Sk\+Canvas}}. The command stream may be played in whole or in part at a later time.

\mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} is an abstract class. \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} may be generated by \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture_recorder}{Sk\+Picture\+Recorder}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_drawable}{Sk\+Drawable}}, or from \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} previously saved to \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_data}{Sk\+Data}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_stream}{Sk\+Stream}}.

\mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} may contain any \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_canvas}{Sk\+Canvas}} drawing command, as well as one or more \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_canvas}{Sk\+Canvas}} matrix or \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_canvas}{Sk\+Canvas}} clip. \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} has a cull \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_rect}{Sk\+Rect}}, which is used as a bounding box hint. To limit \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}} bounds, use \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_canvas}{Sk\+Canvas}} clip when recording or drawing \mbox{\hyperlink{class_sk_picture}{Sk\+Picture}}.

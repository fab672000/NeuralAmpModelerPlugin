\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Speaker Properties. The origin for azimuth is right (as by math conventions dealing with radians). The elevation origin is also right, visualizing a rotation of a circle across the -\/pi/pi axis of the horizontal circle. Thus, an elevation of -\/pi/2 corresponds to bottom, and a speaker standing on the left, and \textquotesingle{}beaming\textquotesingle{} upwards would have an azimuth of -\/pi, and an elevation of pi/2. For user interface representation, grads are more likely to be used, and the origins will obviously \textquotesingle{}shift\textquotesingle{} accordingly.

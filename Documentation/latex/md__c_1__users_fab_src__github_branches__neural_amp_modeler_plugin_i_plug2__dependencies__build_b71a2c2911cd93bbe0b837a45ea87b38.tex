This effect makes copies of its input sound and remixes them to the stereo field using differently modulated delays, thus simulating a effects of nearly identical sound sources. The included effects are\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Flanger
\item Chorus
\item Echo/\+Delay
\item Reverb
\end{DoxyItemize}

The effects output is always stereo, while its input may be either mono or stereo. To minimize C\+PU usage, place the effects at the point in your effects chain where the effects are mono to the left and stereo to the right.\hypertarget{md__c_1__users_fab_src__github_branches__neural_amp_modeler_plugin_i_plug2__dependencies__build_b71a2c2911cd93bbe0b837a45ea87b38_autotoc_md909}{}\doxysection{Parameters}\label{md__c_1__users_fab_src__github_branches__neural_amp_modeler_plugin_i_plug2__dependencies__build_b71a2c2911cd93bbe0b837a45ea87b38_autotoc_md909}

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Convert stream of arbitrary M\+I\+DI bytes into discrete messages.

Parses the incoming bytes and then posts individual messages to the receiver specified in the constructor. Short messages of 1-\/3 bytes will be complete. System Exclusive messages may be posted in pieces.

Resolves Running Status and interleaved System Real-\/\+Time messages.

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_rect}{Sk\+Rect}} holds four Sk\+Scalar coordinates describing the upper and lower bounds of a rectangle. \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_rect}{Sk\+Rect}} may be created from outer bounds or from position, width, and height. \mbox{\hyperlink{struct_sk_rect}{Sk\+Rect}} describes an area; if its right is less than or equal to its left, or if its bottom is less than or equal to its top, it is considered empty.
